### What are the differences between 802.11a and 802.11b? ###



802.11a

	- Frequency band: 5 GHz
	- Maximum Data Rate: 54 Mbps
	- Range: high connectivity due to high frequency but shorter range/coverage
	- Interference: Less interference
	- Modulation: Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiplexing(OFDM)



802.11b

	- Frequency band: 2.4 GHz
	- Maximum Data Rate: 11 Mbps
	- Range: Longer range/coverage
	- Interference: More interference
	- Modulation: Direct Sequence Spread Spectrum(DSSS)
