### What are the basic functionalities of a Wi-Fi Access Point? ###

Wi-Fi Access Point (AP):


Features:
	-It enables wireless devices to connect to the network via radio waves(Establishment of wireless connectivity between devices).
	-APs connect wireless clients to the wired LAN(Router/Switch).
	-It forwards data between the wireless clients and the wired network (or other devices). 
	-APs broadcast SSID, enabling clients to identify the network and receive an IP address.
