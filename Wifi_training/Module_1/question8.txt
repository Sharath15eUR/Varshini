### What is the difference between IEEE and WFA? ###

IEEE:
	- Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers 
	- It is the standards organization responsible for developing and publishing Wi-Fi standards like 802.11.



WFA:
	- Wi-Fi Alliance 
	- It is a non-profit organization that certifies Wi-Fi devices for interoperability. 
	- It ensures that devices comply with Wi-Fi standards and operate well together, regardless of manufacturer.
