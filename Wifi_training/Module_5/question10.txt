### How do 802.11k/v/r work together to provide seamless roaming in enterprise networks? ###


802.11k provides information about nearby APs so the device can make an informed decision about which AP to connect to.

802.11v allows the network to direct devices to the best AP for seamless roaming, minimizing dropped connections.

802.11r speeds up the handoff process between APs by pre-authenticating the device with the new AP, reducing the time to reconnect.

Together, these standards ensure that users experience seamless and reliable connections as they move throughout a large enterprise network.
