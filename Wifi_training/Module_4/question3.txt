### Please list all the MAC layer functionalities in all Management, Control and Data plane ###


-Management Plane:
	-Association and disassociation with APs.
	-Authentication and key management.
	-Scanning and network discovery.
	-Beacon frame transmission for APs.
	-Probe requests and responses for network discovery.



-Control Plane:
	-RTS/CTS for collision avoidance.
	-ACK for frame delivery confirmation.
	-Block ACK for managing large data frames efficiently.
	-Power management.



-Data Plane:
	-Frame forwarding.
	-Fragmentation and reassembly.
	-QoS management for prioritization of traffic.
	-Error correction.
	-Encryption and decryption.
